Trello :

A project management tool to
  - build projects
  - assign task to different users
  - have due dates etc.

it is a collaboration tool that organizes the project into boards
it is a free web based project management tool

Board : Used to assign activities to different individuals and track it
List : A specific area of the project that amounts specific activities
Card : An exact activity to be executed

Labels :

 A badge with the due date will appear on the card.
 • A light grey badge means the card is due more than 24 hours in the future
 • Yellow badge means it's within 24 hours of being due
 • Red badge means the card is due and it will remain red for 24 hours
 • Light pink badge means it's past due,
 • And green badge means it's complete.
